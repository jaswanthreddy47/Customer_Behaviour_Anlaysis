## Overview
The **Customer Behavior Dashboard** is a data analytics project designed to analyze customer purchasing patterns, subscription trends, demographic behavior, and revenue distribution across product categories. The dashboard provides actionable insights that help businesses understand customer behavior and make data-driven decisions.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze overall customer purchasing behavior  
- Identify high-performing product categories  
- Understand the impact of subscription status on customers  
- Study customer spending patterns across different age groups  
- Evaluate customer satisfaction using review ratings  

---

## Key Metrics (KPIs)
- **Total Customers:** ~3,900  
- **Average Purchase Amount:** $59.76  
- **Average Review Rating:** 3.75  

These KPIs provide a quick summary of business performance and customer engagement.

---

## Dashboard Insights

### 1. Customer Subscription Analysis
- **27%** of customers are subscribed  
- **73%** are non-subscribers  
- Indicates a strong opportunity to improve subscription conversions through targeted marketing strategies.

---

### 2. Revenue by Product Category
- **Clothing** generates the highest revenue  
- Followed by **Accessories**, **Footwear**, and **Outerwear**  
- Outerwear contributes the least, suggesting the need for improved promotions or pricing strategies.

---

### 3. Age Group Purchase Behavior
- **Young Adults** contribute the highest total purchase amount  
- Followed by **Middle-aged**, **Adults**, and **Seniors**  
- Young adults are identified as the most valuable customer segment.

---

### 4. Customer Satisfaction
- Average review rating of **3.75**  
- Indicates generally positive customer feedback with scope for improvement.

---
